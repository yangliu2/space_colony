The lighting is functional — white LED panels on the platform structures, supplemented by warm sunlight filtering in from the window strips in the cylinder wall far below.

Style: photorealistic industrial sci-fi, clean functional aesthetic (not dystopian), good workplace lighting, medium shot showing both the platform details and the dramatic view to the rim below.

## Key Physics Details for Accuracy

- Gravity is exactly 0.5g — objects fall at half speed, you can jump twice as high
- This zone is 490m from the axis, 490m from the rim
- The Coriolis effect is stronger here (same ω, but movements feel different)
- Water behaves differently — flatter meniscus, slower pouring
- The "floor" of this platform curves — you're on a ring inside the cylinder
- Looking down, you see the full interior landscape 490m below through open air
- Temperature may differ from rim level (convection patterns in rotating frame)
