# Gravity
Moon have gravity of 0.166 g and Mars have gravity 0.38 g. These planets both have lower gravity than earth. Lower gravity result in multiple problems for humans evolved on earth, including muscle atrophy, bone density loss, heart and blood vessel problem, and most importantly reproductive and developmental concerns. 

To solve all those problem separate is going to be close to impossible. So creating close to 1g gravity is the most likely solution.
