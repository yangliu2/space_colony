## Prompt

Style: photorealistic, dramatic perspective looking down the full length of the cylinder, spacecraft and industrial hardware details, mix of functional engineering and awe-inspiring scale.

## Key Physics Details for Accuracy

- The docking port is at the rotation axis — zero gravity here
- Spacecraft approach along the axis where they don't need to match rotation
- The end cap is structural only — it holds atmospheric pressure, not centrifugal load
- Agriculture pods are OUTSIDE the main cylinder, pressurized separately
- The entire interior rotates relative to the docking port
- Arriving passengers must transfer from the non-rotating dock to the rotating habitat
- The transition from 0g (axis) to 1g (rim) happens via radial elevators
